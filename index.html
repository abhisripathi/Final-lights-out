Development Process and Insights

The journey of creating the "Revenge of Lights Out" game was an enlightening experience that involved a blend of creative decision-making and problem-solving.
The primary goal was to develop an interactive and engaging game that offers a fun challenge to players.

Creative Choices:

  • Game Design: The decision to use a 5x5 grid was made to provide a moderate level of difficulty, balancing complexity and accessibility for players of varying skill levels.
  • User Interface: A minimalist design approach was chosen for the user interface to keep the focus on the gameplay. This choice was also in line with modern design trends favoring simplicity and usability.
  • Feedback Mechanism: The use of a modal window to announce the game's completion was intended to give players a clear and satisfying indication of their success.

Challenges and Solutions:

  • Randomizing the Board: One of the initial challenges was implementing an effective way to shuffle the board at the start of each game.
    This was overcome by introducing a randomization function that ensures a new experience in each game session.
  • Ensuring Playability: Ensuring that the game was always solvable after shuffling was a complex task. This issue was addressed through careful testing and tweaking of the shuffling algorithm.

Insights Gained:

  • User-Centric Design: The importance of keeping the user experience at the forefront became clear. Simple, intuitive gameplay and interface design significantly enhance player engagement.
  • Testing is Crucial: Rigorous testing was instrumental in identifying and solving gameplay and usability issues, highlighting the importance of thorough testing in game development.
